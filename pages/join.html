title: Connect with the Project

Faculty

Engelhard faculty fellows use a curriculum infusion approach to link academic course content to health and well-being topics through readings, presentations, discussions led by campus health professionals, and reflective writing assignments. To view examples of past course collaborations and thematic topics, visit our Course Profilespage.

If you are interested in learning more about participation in the Engelhard faculty fellowship, please see the Faculty Fellowship Requirements.
To apply to participate as a fellow and teach your course as an Engelhard course for Fall 2017 please fill out the Course Proposal Form.

Campus Resource Professionals

Interested in connecting with faculty and supporting the Engelhard Project through course visits? Please contact a member of the Project Team or email us at engelhardproject@georgetown.edu.

General Interest

While our Frequently Asked Questions page offers more details on the Project, if you have any additional questions or would like to learn more, please contact a member of the Project Team or email us at engelhardproject@georgetown.edu.
