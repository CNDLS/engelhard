title: Join the Project

Engelhard faculty fellows use a curriculum infusion approach to link academic course content to health and well-being topics through readings, presentations, discussions led by campus health professionals, and reflective writing assignments.

If you are interested in applying to be an Engelhard Faculty Fellow, please contact the Project Team.
