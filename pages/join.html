title: Join the Project

Engelhard faculty fellows use a curriculum infusion approach to link academic course content to health and well-being topics through readings, presentations, discussions led by campus health professionals, and reflective writing assignments.

If you are interested in learning more about participation in the Engelhard faculty fellowship, please see the Faculty Fellowship Requirements. To apply to participate as a fellow and teach your course as an Engelhard course for Spring 2016, please fill out the Course Proposal Form.

If you have any questions about the project or would like to learn more, please contact a member of the Project Team.
