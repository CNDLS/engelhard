Engelhard Safety Net Training:

Georgetown University's Office of Student Outreach and Support maintains a robust “safety net" for students who experience complex, personal difficulty. Each semester, new Engelhard Faculty Fellows and/or Teaching Assistants are required to attend a campus “Safety Net Training,” which is offered through a collaboration with Student Affairs, CAPS, and Health Education Services. To learn about the next Safety Net Training, contact a member of the Project Team or email us at engelhardproject@georgetown.edu.
